What is your Website Domain?



Don't have a domain name?
No problem. We recommend using GoDaddy to purchase your domain registration.

1. Visit GoDaddy.com
2. Search to find an available domain name
3. Buy your domain name
4. Don't signup for hosting yet, we will provide hosting as a part of our service

Follow the Instructions below to Setup Your Nameservers


Don't worry, it's easy!
Simply login to your GoDaddy account and complete the following steps:

[Seriously should do a video]
1. Go to "My Account"
2. Click on Domains > Then Manage
3. Under Settings, look for your Nameservers and click Manage
4. The Nameserver Settings window will pop up. Please select Setup Type of Custom, and click on Edit Nameservers
5. You will need to add three nameservers as shown below (NS1.Dreamhost.com, NS2.Dreamhost.com, and NS3.Dreamhost.com)
6. Click OK and you are done!

Are you having trouble setting up your nameservers? Shoot us an email at support@whatever.com and we will help you out.
